## Abstract
- Given [[Set|Set]] `A` and `B`. The [[#Cardinality of a Set]] must be the same
- **First way to prove:**
$$
A = B \leftrightarrow (A \subseteq B) \land (B \subseteq A)
$$
- **Second way to prove:**
$$
A = B \leftrightarrow \forall x (x \in A \leftrightarrow x \in B)
$$
