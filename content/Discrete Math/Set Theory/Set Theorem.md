## Subset Relation
---
- Theorem 6.2.1
#### Inclusion of Intersection
$$
A \cap B \subseteq A
$$
$$
A \cap B \subseteq B
$$

#### Inclusion in Union
$$
A \subseteq A \cup B
$$
$$
B \subseteq A \cup B
$$

#### Transitive Property of Subsets
$$
(A \subseteq B) \land (B \subseteq C) \rightarrow A \subseteq C
$$
