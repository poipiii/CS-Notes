## Abstract
---
$$
A \subseteq B \leftrightarrow \forall x (x \in A \rightarrow x \in B)
$$

### Proper Subset
$$
A \subsetneq B \leftrightarrow  (A \subseteq B) \land (A \ne B)
$$

### Superset
$$
B \supseteq A
$$
- $A$ is a [[#Subset]] of $B$
