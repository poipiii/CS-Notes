## Abstract
---
- The power set of [[Set|Set]] $A$ is all the possible [[Subnet]] of $A$
- Given $A = \{x, y\}$,  $\mathcal{P}(A) = \{\emptyset, \{x\}, \{y\}, \{x,y\}\}$

### Theorem 6.3.1
- The cardinality of [[Power Set]] of finite set is $2^{cardinality~of ~the~finite~set}$
